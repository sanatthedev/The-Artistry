BLENDER PROJECT 1

PROJECT


Subject:
1. The main focus of the image is a stack of colorful donuts on a plate.


Donuts:
1. There are multiple donuts stacked on top of each other.
2. Each donut has a different colored frosting: red, green, brown, purple, etc.
3. The frosting on each donut is topped with colorful sprinkles.


Plate:
1. The donuts are placed on a round, light-colored plate.


Background:
1. The background shows a kitchen setting with a marble or tiled wall.
2. There are two white pots in the background:
3. One pot contains a green leafy plant.
4. The other pot holds wooden cooking utensils such as spoons and spatulas.


Lighting:
1. The lighting in the scene is soft and natural, similar to sunlight.
2. There are subtle reflections and shadows, adding to the realism of the scene.


Materials and Textures:
1. The frosting on the donuts has a glossy texture, reflecting light realistically.
2. The sprinkles are small and colorful, with slight variations in size and color.
3. The plate has a smooth, ceramic-like texture.
4. The pots and the utensils have realistic textures and materials.
