PROJECT

BLENDER PROJECT 1:


1. This 3D render from Blender depicts a plate of colorful donuts stacked on top of each other.


2. The donuts are frosted in various vibrant colors such as red, green, purple, and brown, each adorned with multicolored sprinkles.


3. The scene is set on a marble countertop with a blurred background featuring potted plants and wooden utensils in a container, adding a homey, kitchen-like atmosphere.


4. The lighting is soft, highlighting the glossy texture of the donuts and creating a realistic, appealing presentation.
